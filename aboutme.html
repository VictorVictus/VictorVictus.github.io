My name is Víctor Puche i Alonso. I was born in the vibrant city of Barcelona, but currently call Palau-Solità i Plegamans my home, a charming town near the city. I am currently immersed in my studies of Industrial Electronics and Automation Engineering, a discipline that fuels my passion for innovation and technological progress.

Community commitment

From a young age, I have felt a strong commitment to my community and its well-being. For the past two years, I have actively participated in various community initiatives, acting as an intermediary between local residents and political authorities to improve everyone's quality of life. Through this, we have successfully engaged citizens during key municipal events.

These experiences have provided me with local and regional contacts, bridging political parties and neighborhood associations to relay citizen concerns directly to the administrations. Currently, I serve as the financial secretary of a neighborhood association, managing funds used for efficient citizen mobilization when necessary.

University representation

Recently, I had the honor of being elected to represent students in the cloister of the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya. I ran as an independent candidate and won my parliamentary representation thanks to the support of my peers. This opportunity allows me to actively participate in university decision-making and defend our interests.

Empathy and communication

My interest in empathy and effective communication stems from personal experiences, including moments of adversity in my childhood. These events taught me the importance of understanding different perspectives and collaborating to find solutions.

Professional philosophy

In the workplace, I firmly believe in creating an inclusive environment where individuals feel valued and can contribute their ideas openly. An organizational culture based on mutual respect and collaboration is essential for the success of any company.

Regarding economics, I am convinced that public-private collaboration can generate powerful synergies driving economic growth and social well-being. I believe in balancing business interests with general societal welfare, as companies ultimately exist to identify and solve societal problems in exchange for a monetary reward.
